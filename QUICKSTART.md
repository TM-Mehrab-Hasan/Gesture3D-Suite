## âš™ï¸ Troubleshooting

### Gestures Not Detected?
1. **Improve Lighting** - Face a window or turn on lights
2. **Clean Background** - Avoid busy backgrounds
3. **Camera Distance** - Keep hand 1-2 feet from camera
4. **Contrast** - Wear dark clothes with light background (or vice versa)
5. **Try Chrome** - Best compatibility

### Low FPS / Laggy?
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Close GPU-intensive applications
3. Reduce browser window size
4. Update graphics drivers

### Camera Not Working?
1. Check browser permissions (Settings â†’ Privacy â†’ Camera)
2. Make sure no other app is using the camera
3. Try a different browser
4. Restart your browser

### Gestures Triggering Accidentally?
- Move hand slower
- Hold gestures longer (system requires 3 frames confirmation)
- Increase distance from camera slightly
